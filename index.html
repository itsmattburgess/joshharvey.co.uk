Josh Harvey

Screen and stage actor and clown, Josh Harvey, is known for his roles in sketch comedy 'The Not Very Good Show' and feature film 'A Reasonably Good Bloke'. Whilst one part of the comedy troupe The Lampoons, Josh has also performed across UK theatres in No1 tours and The Sonning Mill Theatre. You may recently have caught him in commercials for BBC’s ‘Britain’s Best Home cook’ and Paperchase’s ‘Cupid’.
